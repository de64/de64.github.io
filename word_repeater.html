Repeat words at optimal time.

Based on what spaced repetition technique.

Currently the aim for the system is to be used in the morning and in the evening. If you recall correctly the word, its frequency is increased twice; Minimal frequency is 12 hours.

As you encounter words which are not familiar, add them in the field below, and save them using "Add" button. Click on "Next" button to load a next word to recall. Provide feedback on whether you remembered the meaning of the word or not using "Recalled" or "Forgot" buttons. The frequency of word appearance is adjusted accordingly.
